2015

❮ ❯


Electric Picnic festival in 2015 attracted 50'000 people and was headlined by Florence and The Machine, Blur, Sam Smith, Grace Jones and The Underworld. It's proven to be the biggest Electric Picnic to date. Other performing acts included Tame Impala, George Ezra, MK, Gavin James and many many more.

Underworld took the crowd by storm as they played the first act followed by Grace Jones singing and dancing with a hoola hoop. Damon Albarn from Blur rocked the stage with 'Girls and Boys' with a stage leap involved followed by Sam Smith serenading the entire crowd into silence.

The theme for Theater of Food this year was Food For Life! which offered a mouth-watering menu for all festival-goers.
