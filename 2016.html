2016

❮ ❯


The banger headline acts of 2016 included Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds, The Chemical Brothers, Lcd Soundsystem, Lana Del Rey and New Order. This year we attracted a total of 55'000 people, a strong step up from last year! Other acts included The 1975, Years & Years, James Bay, Walking on Cars, Oh Wonder, Glass Animals and tonnes more.

The crowd warmed up by hearing The Chemical Brothers kick off the show and seeing the amazing visuals that they provided. Lcd Soundsystem followed suit returned for yet another year producing a high energy performance which led way to another performance by the highly anticipated Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds who gave one hell of a performance. The show finally closed with the amazing Lana Del Rey giving a mezmerizing performance.

We also had some amazing acts on The Comedy Stage such as Rubberbandits, Dylan Moran, David O'Doherty and was headlined by Reginald D Hunter. Other acts included Aisling Bea, Joe Rooney, Eric Laylor and Deirdre O'Kane.
