Info





Campsite times:

Early entry ticket holders - 4pm August 29th to 1pm September 2nd.

Campervan site - 4pm August 29th to 1pm September 2nd.

General/family campsite - 9am August 29th to 1pm September 2nd.

Family camping:

To avail of this campsite festival goers must be accompanied by children aged 12 and under. Space is restricted which is why this area is reserved for families only. Children may require ID for proof of age.

Boutique camping:

The Boutique camping site is a luxury site containing the Pink Moon Village and Harvest Moon Boutique. From cosy lodges to wide bell tents the Harvest Moon campsite offers a range of luxurious facilities and accomodations for those looking to soak up the fesitval experience in total style.

Kip'n'Go is another site within the Boutique campsite offering pre-pitched tents available for 2 people per tent from €7.5 per night. We promote this system as a means of helping the environment by avoiding post-festival tent ditching!

Emergency access lanes:

In event of an emergency these lanes will be visible as they're marked by scaffolding poles and festoon lighting. Please be aware of your campsite name and if lost during an emergency please ask a member of staff in your area about where to go.




Gate opening times:

Friday's opening times are between 12pm-4am while Saturday and Sunday's times are from 8am-4am.

Camping:

Parking is available to festival goers for free, roadside parking is not permitted.

If you hold a weekend ticket you will be provided with access to the Red Car Parks labeled A, B, C on the Abbeyleix Road and V, W, X, Y, Z located in the Green Car Park off the N80.

Bus/taxi parking:

A drop off point for all taxi's and buses is positioned on the Stradbally/Portlaoise Road.

Routes:

• M7 Southbound – All event traffic travelling south on the M7 will exit at Junction 16 and on to Broomfield Roundabout on the Stradbally road. Follow the signage. • M7 Northbound - Exit at Junction 17 and follow signage. • Via Portlaoise – Use relief road and follow signage. • M8 Northbound - Exit at Junction 3 and follow signage through Ballacolla, Abbeyleix, Ballyroan and on to site • N80 – via Carlow – proceed straight to Stradbally. • R428 – via Athy – proceed straight to Stradbally.
